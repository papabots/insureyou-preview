Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

We respect your privacy. We only collect the information we need to find you the best insurance, we keep it secure, and we never sell your personal data to random marketing lists.

Below is the full legal breakdown of how Insureyou Advisory manages your personal information in compliance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Introduction

This policy details how Insureyou Advisory collects, holds, uses, and discloses your personal information. By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

What is "Personal Information"? Information that identifies you, such as name, address, and contact details.

What is "Sensitive Information"? Health records, genetic data, racial or ethnic origin, and criminal history. We often collect this to secure your insurance coverage.

2. What We Collect

We only collect information reasonably necessary to provide you with insurance advice and products:

  • General Details: Name, address, date of birth, gender, marital status, and occupation.
  • Contact Details: Phone number, email address, mailing address.
  • Financial Details: Bank account or credit card info, income, assets, liabilities.
  • Sensitive/Health Information: Medical history, lifestyle factors, family medical history.

3. How We Collect It

We collect information directly from you whenever possible, for example:

  • Fill out a quote form on our website.
  • Speak with one of our advisors over the phone.
  • Complete an application form.

We may also collect information from third parties with your consent, such as medical practitioners, insurers (for past claims history), or your accountant/financial planner.

4. Why We Collect It

We use your information to:

  • Get you covered: Assess risks and provide quotes.
  • Manage your policy: Help with applications, updates, renewals.
  • Handle claims: Fight for your payout when needed.
  • Comply with the law: Fulfill legal obligations under Australian law.

5. Who We Share It With

We do not sell your information. However, to provide insurance, we may share your details with:

  • Insurers & Reinsurers to obtain quotes and coverage.
  • Medical Professionals to verify health information if required.
  • Service Providers (IT support, CRM, auditors) under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal & Regulatory Bodies if required by law (e.g., ATO, Courts, Centrelink).

6. Keeping Your Data Secure

We use physical and electronic measures to protect your data from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorized access. This includes secure office facilities, firewalls, encryption, and strict staff access controls.

7. Access and Correction

You can request access to the personal information we hold and request corrections if anything is inaccurate. Contact us using the details at the bottom of this page.

8. Direct Marketing

We may use your contact details to provide updates about your policy or products. You can opt-out anytime via "unsubscribe" or by contacting us directly.

9. Complaints

If you think we have mishandled your privacy, please let us know. We want to fix it.

Step 1: Contact Us – Contact our Privacy Officer using the details below. We will investigate and respond within 30 days.

Step 2: External Review – If not satisfied, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC):
