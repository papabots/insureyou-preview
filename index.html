Income Protection

Replaces up to 70% of your salary if you’re sick or injured and can't work. It keeps the mortgage paid and food on the table while you recover.

Life Insurance

A lump sum for your family if you pass away or become terminally ill. It ensures your loved ones are financially secure, no matter what happens.

Total & Permanent Disability (TPD)

A payout if you are permanently disabled and can never work again. It replaces your future income so you can afford medical costs and adapt to your new reality.

Trauma (Critical Illness)

Cash in hand if you are diagnosed with a major illness like cancer, heart attack, or stroke. Use it to pay for treatment, clear debts, or just take time off to heal.
