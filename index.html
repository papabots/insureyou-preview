FAQ

Do I actually need personal insurance?

If you are financially independent, the answer is usually yes. Ask yourself these four questions:

  • Income Protection: Could I pay rent and buy food if I couldn't work for 6 months?
  • Life Insurance: Would my family be financially okay if I wasn't around tomorrow?
  • Disability: Could I afford to retire today if an injury stopped me from ever working again?
  • Trauma: Could I afford huge medical bills and time off work to fight cancer or a heart attack?

How much cover do I need?

There is no "one size fits all." It depends on your debts, family situation, and lifestyle. We help you run the numbers so you are fully covered for the worst-case scenario without paying for fluff you don't need.

How much does it cost?

It depends on your age, health, job, and the cover you choose. After a 10-minute chat, we can scan the market to find you the best value. Using us doesn't cost you a cent extra; insurers pay us.

How do I know I‚Äôm getting the best price?

We compare 10 of Australia‚Äôs top insurers, covering ~91% of the quality market. We find the "sweet spot"‚Äîaffordable, reliable policies without unnecessary exclusions.

Can I pay via my Super fund?

Yes. You can pay for Life, TPD, and Income Protection through Super.

  • Pro: Saves your monthly cash flow and often gives a 15% tax rebate.
  • Con: Reduces retirement savings (can be topped up manually). We help weigh the pros & cons.

Default Super cover is generic and not medically underwritten upfront. We set up tailored policies so you know exactly where you stand.
