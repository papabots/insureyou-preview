Information about our insurance products, their features, benefits, and risks.

What is a PDS?

A Product Disclosure Statement (PDS) provides detailed information about a specific insurance product. It helps you understand the product and make informed decisions.

Key Sections in Our PDS

  • Product Overview
  • Coverage Details
  • Exclusions and Limitations
  • Premiums and Fees
  • How to Make a Claim
  • Cooling-off Period

Accessing Our PDS

You can request a copy of the PDS for any insurance product via our website or by contacting our team directly.

Important Information

Please read the PDS carefully before applying for an insurance product. Ensure it meets your needs and consider seeking independent advice if required.
